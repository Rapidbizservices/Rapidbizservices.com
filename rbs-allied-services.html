    Our Allied Services

    Allied Services – Enhancing Every Step of Your PCBA Journey

    At Rapid Business Services LLC, we offer a range of precision-driven allied services that complement and strengthen our PCBA design, prototyping, and manufacturing operations across our facilities in the USA, China, and India. These services ensure that every build—no matter the volume or complexity—is handled with utmost accuracy, consistency, and reliability.

    Integrated Allied Capabilities Include:

    • Meticulous Visual Inspection
      Every component and assembly undergoes a thorough visual check to identify cosmetic defects, placement accuracy, and potential process issues early in the build cycle.
    • Seamless Kitting of Parts
      We prepare fully verified kits—sorted, labeled, and aligned with your BOM—to ensure smooth and efficient assembly operations with minimal downtime.
    • Basic Quality Checks
      Our trained operators perform dimension and form checks, ensuring components meet required tolerances and basic specifications before entering production.
    • Precision Measurement (Vernier & Micrometer Checks)
      We use calibrated verniers and micrometers to perform dimensional verification of mechanical and electromechanical components, enhancing accuracy and fit.
    • Third-Party Quality Inspection Coordination
      For customers who require independent validation, we facilitate third-party quality inspections and certifications—ensuring your standards are met, wherever your product is built.

    By embedding these services into our workflow, we deliver seamless quality assurance and operational readiness that support every phase of your electronics product lifecycle—from prototyping in Laguna Niguel (USA) to volume production in Pune (India) and component sourcing support in China.

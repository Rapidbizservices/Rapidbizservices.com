    PCBA Design, Development, Prototyping and Manufacturing

    Rapid Business Services

    At Rapid Business Services LLC, we provide end-to-end electronics assembly solutions—from design and prototyping to development and manufacturing. With operations in both the U.S., Mexico, China and India, we deliver high-quality results for customers requiring medium-volume production and rapid-turnaround prototypes.

    Our Facilities

    • Prototyping Facility: Laguna Niguel, California, USA
    • Production Facility: Tepexpan, Mexico, Shenzhen, China and Pune, Maharashtra, India

    We are equipped to handle both development-stage builds and scaled production while maintaining a strong focus on quality, flexibility, and customer service.

    Our Capabilities

    We specialize in the complete lifecycle of electronics product development, including:

    • Printed Circuit Board Assembly (PCBA)
    • Surface-Mount Technology (SMT) Assembly
    • Through-hole and mixed-technology assembly
    • Functional and In-Circuit Testing (ICT)
    • Design for Manufacturability (DFM) analysis
    • Component sourcing and BOM optimization

    Key SMT Equipment

    Our SMT line includes industry-leading machinery to ensure speed, precision, and reliability:

    • Pick and Place Machines (Yamaha, Juki, Fuji)
    • 8-zone and 10-zone Reflow Ovens
    • Solder Paste Printers with 2D/3D SPI
    • Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Systems
    • X-ray Inspection for BGA and QFN Components
    • Wave Soldering Machines for through-hole assemblies

    Why Choose Us

    • Fast and flexible prototyping
    • Cost-effective medium-volume production
    • Global delivery model
    • Commitment to quality and customer satisfaction
